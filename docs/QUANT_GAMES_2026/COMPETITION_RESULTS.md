## Future Competition Advice

### For Next Year's Participants
1. **Start with simplicity:** Baseline first, optimize later
2. **Validate early and often:** Bugs found early = easy fixes
3. **Symbol-specific matters:** Don't force one strategy on all
4. **Transaction costs kill:** Test with 2x costs for safety
5. **Rule 12 is serious:** Check trade counts continuously
6. **Sleep is optional:** But coffee is mandatory ‚òï
7. **Document everything:** Helps debugging and impresses judges
8. **Crisis plan:** Reserve 3 hours for final bug fixes

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- ‚ùå Overfitting on training data
- ‚ùå Ignoring transaction costs
- ‚ùå Not checking trade count requirements
- ‚ùå Testing only in-sample performance
- ‚ùå Complex strategies without validation
- ‚ùå Submitting at last minute without buffer
